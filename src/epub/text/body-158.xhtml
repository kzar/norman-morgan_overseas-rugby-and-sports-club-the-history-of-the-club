CRICKET SECTION 1976

The Annual General Meeting was initially held at the Club House on the 19th February, 1976, but due to an error in procedure it was declared null and void. The meeting was reconvened on the 25th March, 1976.

The following Officials were elected:-

Chairman M. Caseley
Hon. Secretary G. Davey
Hon. Treasurer P. Marchant
Hon. Social Secretary J. Hughes
Team Captain M. Newton
Vice-Captain D. Walker
'B' Team Captain J. Robinson

The season opened on the 24th April with the 6 a-side Competition. Overseas entered 4 teams including a team of boys from Tal Handaq as Overseas 'C'. Our strongest team, Overseas '1', succeeded in reaching the semi final when we were beaten by 41 Cdo, the eventual winners.

The final of the Government Tourist Board Trophy for 1975, between the Malta Sports Club and 41 Commando, which had, through force of circumstances, to be left over until May, 1976, was won by the Malta Sports Club.

The Single Wicket and the Double Wicket Competitions were held on the 3/4 July and the 14/15 July respectively. The Double Wicket was won by Ray Berrington and his son. The B.F.B.S. Trophy was held on the 27th June.

Overseas 2nd XI, under the Captaincy of Jim Robinson, played 3 matches, winning against the Malta Sports Club and Tal Handaq, and losing to the Navy.
