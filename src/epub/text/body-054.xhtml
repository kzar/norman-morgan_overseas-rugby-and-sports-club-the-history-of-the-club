REVISION OF RULES

Rule 11 was revised at the A.G.M. to make membership of the Club the same for Playing and Social Members at 10/- per season.

Altered Rule Read:-

  • 11 Membership
    • (a) Playing Members Annual Subscription 10/-
    • (b) Social Members Annual Subscription 10/-
    • (c) Student Members Annual Subscription 2/6

a. b. c. have full privileges.

PLAYING SEASON

The playing results for 1962/63 gave the Club its best season for several years, although according to the Team Captain, Mr. Louis Thacker, they gave little indication of the team's potential. The season was started with a new set of jerseys. The team played with great confidence until beset with injuries to key players over the Christmas period, winning 5 of the first 6 matches. At this stage the points tally was 107 for and 33 against. The Captain was one of the injured players and Paul Flack, Vice Captain, took over the Captaincy.
