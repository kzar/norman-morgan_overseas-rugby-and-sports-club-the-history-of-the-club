PLAYING SEASON

The 1964/65 season started off with two trial matches, the normal Overseas start of the season Trial Match and a Demonstration/Teaching Match under the new rules coming into force that season. This season the Overseas Trial Match was held at Ta' Qali, versus R.A.F. Luqa, on the 31st October, 1964. The Demonstration Match was organized by the Army (Malta) R.F.U. and all arrangements were made by their Hon. Secretary, Major J.W.S. Dominy. Overseas were invited to attend. The Team Captains of all invited Clubs forwarded the names of 5 players and from these names two XVs were selected and two Captains nominated. Overseas was represented by Messrs. J. Handford, J. Why, P. Henshall, D. Henry and I. O'Brien. The Malta Rugby Referees Panel appointed four referees to take this match and thereby gave the referees an opportunity to try their hand under the new laws. The Demonstration Match was held at the Marsa on the 7th November, 1964.
