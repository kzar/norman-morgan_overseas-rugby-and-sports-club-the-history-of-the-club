Finances

At the end of 1976/77 the Hon. General Treasurer, Mr. J. Andrews, was able to report that available funds amounted to £M225.13, and that the U.K. balance stood at £123.37. This was attributed not only to the healthy profit from the Bar of £M175.61, a figure considerably higher than in recent years, but also to all social functions being financially successful. The financial position of the Club was described as being very sound, and in his end of year report the President was able to thank James Andrews for his meticulous book-keeping and his desire to sort out problems at any time.

In addition to the above, the sum of £M172 was still owing to the Club from the sale of T.Shirts. This was a project in which Jeff Bonner had 200 White T.Shirts printed with the name of Overseas Rugby & Sports Club, Malta, a Maltese Cross incorporating the initials ORSC, and a picture of the Maltese Islands. It was not only financially sound and the shirts popular, but it was a means of spreading the word about the Club in Malta, and in the U.K.

Membership

In 1976/77, a difficult year in which the withdrawal of the British Forces and U.K. Based Civilians was beginning to show in the Club's membership strength, the Membership Secretary, Mr. Alan Latham, was able to report a fully paid up membership of 77, including 5 students. A difficult job in difficult time, added to the fact that due to the relaxed nature of rugby in Malta, it was virtually impossible to insist all players were members. These figures compare with previous years as follows:- 

        1973/74    96 members including 25 students
        1974/75    87 members including 1 student
        1975/76    130 members including no students

Over the years the task of Membership Secretary had almost always been carried out by the Hon. Secretary, and it was not until 1973/74, when the membership increased on Overseas becoming a Sports Club, that it was accepted as necessary for one man to be in sole charge of membership. In 1975/76 the rules of the Club were amended to add the Membership Secretary to the list of full members of the General Committee.
