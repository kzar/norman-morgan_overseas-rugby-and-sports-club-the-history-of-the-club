Overseas was represented in the Malta Select XV by Brian
Charnley and by David Schembri as reserve. Energoinvest fielded
an unchanged side. After the running game by Energoinvest
against Overseas, and knowing the strength of the Malta team,
mostly 41 Cdo, a large crowd turned up expecting a good game.
They were not disappointed, but with most threequarter movements
being stopped by the opposing cover, it became an uncompromising
trial of strength up forward, and so scrappy at times. In the
second half the strength of the Malta Select's forwards gradually
wore their opponents down.

At the beginning of the season Overseas yet again attempted
to interest the Maltese Colleges in rugby. Mr. J. Sharp, Rugby
Section Chairman, first approached the National Sports Board
and then wrote direct to the Rectors of St. Edward's, De La Salle
and St. Aloysius College. Mr. Sharp pointed out that we would
like to see the game of rugby established in Malta before the
British presence was withdrawn in 1979. This produced immediate
response from St. Edward's College and by agreement between the
College, the N.S.B., and S.C.S. Tal Handaq, pupils from the
College trained with pupils from Tal Handaq during the latters
games periods on Friday afternoons at the Marsa. The pupils
