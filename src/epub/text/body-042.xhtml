NOTES:

Angelo, the Bar Steward, will be on the premises half an hour before opening time and is responsible for having the bar ready for opening. He leaves the premises half an hour after closing and is responsible for leaving the glasses clean and the bottles in crates and clear.

SERVICE MEMBERS OF THE BAR COMMITTEE

These members are requested to help behind the bar on Wednesdays when necessary, and to help the duty bar member when asked to do so.
