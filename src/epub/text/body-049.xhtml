PLAYING SEASON

The Team Captain, Mr. R. McGillivray, in his short report
on the playing season of 1961/62 made no reference to the
playing activities but expressed his view that in fielding
a XV according to the fixture list the Overseas more than
fulfilled their main object, i.e. to encourage the playing of
Rugby Football in Malta.

The President, Mr. G.H.L. Lord, in his address, gave a
brief outline of the many and varied highlights of the season
saying it had been the best in recent years on and off the
field. The best attended match from the point of spectators,
according to the President, was undoubtedly the game against
the French cruiser 'De Grasse', the French Mediterranean Fleet
champions, on the 26th November, 1961. Overseas fielded an
invitation XV for this game and did well to draw 3 points
each with their opponents. Overseas also fielded a Club side
on the same day against H.M.S. Tiger and lost 0-6.

A point to note is that during this season we received an
invitation from the Secretary Army Rugby Union Malta, Major K.
Perkins, MBE, DFC, RA, to enter a team in the Inter Service
Seven-a-side Competition held at the Marsa on the 11th February,
1962. In fact we entered two teams although there is no record
of the result.
