Club Members in 1954/55. This was a splendidly worked badge in gold and different coloured wire thread, and made to order by an elderly Maltese lady who was renowned for similar work for Service badges. The motif depicted a rugby ball in the top left corner over 'Seas' in the bottom right corner, with a thick band of blue, bordered each side by a thin white stripe (Overseas colours), going from top right to bottom left. In the middle of the seas was a Maltese Cross in Maltese national colours, one half of the cross red and the other half white.

In 1975/76 this design was used to produce a wooden plaque, a photo of which is shown at the back of this history.
