At the end of January, 1975, one of our most popular
and 'colourful' characters, namely, Usain Nasuwale, known
to all as Jim, left Malta. Jim, a Fijian, always with a
great sense of humour and with an interest in all sports,
gave a great deal of his time in representing and coaching
the Junior Members in rugby. At the end of the season we lost
many Rugby Section Members, including Peter Joenring, Vice-
Captain and Social Secretary, Dave Snaith, Grounds Manager
and referee, and Jim Glover, Tours Secretary and all rounds
sportsman. All men hard to replace, both as Officials and
and players, and for his ability on the field perhaps none
more than Jim Glover. Approximately 15 Section Members left
Malta at the end of the season or during the close season,
too many to mention all by name, but all a loss to the Club.
