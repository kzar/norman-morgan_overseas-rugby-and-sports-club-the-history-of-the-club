ST. EDWARDS COLLEGE

At this point I would like to put on record the sustained efforts over a number of years, with corresponding good results, which St. Edwards College has given, both to support Overseas, and to promote rugby in their own right in Malta. I think special praise must go to Father Ferro as the driving force. There is no doubt that De la Salle and St. Aloysius Colleges also played their part, but without the same results. If ever rugby is revived in Malta it will have to be with the three Colleges, the University and the Armed Forces as the core.
