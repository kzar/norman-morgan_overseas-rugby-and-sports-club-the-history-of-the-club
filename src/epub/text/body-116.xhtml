SOCIAL EVENTS 1972/73

Except for the Annual Dinner Dance, held at the New Villa Rosa, St. George's Bay, on the 5th May, 1973, the only social events of the season were the film evenings at the Club House.

The film evenings starting on the 8th February proved a success and a good source of revenue at the bar. Once again all the equipment and films were provided by Jack Sharp.

The Annual Dinner Dance, although financially a failure, was an enjoyable event, in pleasant surroundings.

The Club House closed during the summer months.
