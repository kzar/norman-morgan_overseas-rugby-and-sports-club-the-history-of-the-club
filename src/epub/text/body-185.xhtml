The Overseas Soccer Section has over the years relied
very heavily on the more 'mature' and 'experienced' players
and many members of the team had thought that he had 'hung
up his boots' for good before arriving in Malta and letting
it be known that he 'used to' play football. This is further
illustrated in our remarkably consistent average which in
1973 was 33 and in 1978 had crept up to 34, unusually high
figures for a football team to say the least. Nevertheless,
this could well be a factor that has made the soccer team
such an enjoyable one to play for, and one which has had
considerable success during its 15 years existence. It is
particularly pleasant then to see on this last season the
Corinthians providing such an excellent farewell performance.
