10th May, 1982

Dear Mr Fox,

Thank you so much for your letter which has been forwarded to me by George Attard and informs us of the demise of the Malta Rugby Club.

We do fully appreciate the difficulties under which you have been operating during the past few years and were very conscious of the immense amount of energy and work which was involved in keeping going.

It is always sad when Clubs of this nature have to wind up their affairs but we do sincerely hope that the game will have been given a good foundation in Malta and that some of the schools will continue to play and hopefully in due course the Club may arise again.

Thank you so much also for the donation to the Rugby Football Union. We will try and think of some way in which we can use this money to commemorate the activities of your Club so that it will not be entirely lost to our records and also in the minds of rugby people throughout the world.

I do sincerely hope that we shall have an opportunity of meeting you if and when you come to the United Kingdom and would welcome you at Twickenham whenever that may be.

Yours sincerely,


Bob Weighill
