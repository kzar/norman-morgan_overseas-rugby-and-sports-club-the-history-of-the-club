  1. That the Club colours be navy blue and white hoops.
  2. That the Officers of the Club shall consist of The
    President, Chairman, Hon. Secretary, Hon. Treasurer, Hon.
    Fixture Secretary, Hon. Team Secretary, Team Captains. These
    Officers to hold office for one year and be elected by members
    at an Annual General Meeting.
  3. That the management of the Club shall be entrusted to
    a General Committee consisting of such Officers as shall be
    elected at the Annual General Meeting. Liaison with the
    representatives of the three Services shall be effected.
  4. That the General Committee shall have the power to
    administer the funds and property in execution of the Club's
    aims. No expenditure or debts shall be contracted other than
    by resolution and majority vote.
  5. That the General Committee shall have the powers to
    fill any vacancies which may arise in their number during the
    course of the year, and have full power to co-opt as may be
    found expedient.
  6. That the General Committee shall have the power to
    delegate any of its duties to such other Committees as may
    be necessary.
  7. A quorum for a General Committee shall be two thirds
    of the Committee.
  8. Annual General Meeting to be held in October. Items
    to be included are Statement of Accounts, Report of the
    Committee and election of Officers for the ensuing year.
  9. Members to be given 14 days notice of General Meetings.
    Items for inclusion on the Agenda to reach the Hon. Secretary
    7 days prior to the meeting. Extraordinary General Meetings
    may be called by ten or more members or by a majority vote
    of the General Committee.
  10. That the sanction of two thirds of the members present
    and voting at the Annual or Extra-Ordinary General Meeting is
    required for the passing of any resolution to alter the rules.
    Any other resolution shall be passed by a simple majority.
  11. Membership
    • (a) Full Members: Annual Subscription 10/-
      and match fee of 1/-
    • (b) Non-Playing Member and Associate Member:
      Annual subscription 5/-
    • (c) Temporary Membership 1/- per match.
    Members referred to in paragraphs (a) and (b) above
    have full privileges.
