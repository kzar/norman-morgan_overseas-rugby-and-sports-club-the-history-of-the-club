Election of Life Member - David Taylor

On the 3rd April, 1976, David Taylor was honoured by being elected an Hon. Life Member of Overseas Rugby and Sports Club. Speaking on behalf of the General Committee, Mr. J. Sharp, Chairman of the Rugby Section, said that David had served Overseas in many capacities since he joined the Club on his arrival in Malta in 1970, both as a player and in an Official capacity, not least in his final year as President.

David had played with credit for 6 years for the 1st teams of all four Sections, Rugby, Soccer, Hockey and Cricket, also playing a great part in training youth and teaching pupils at the Maltese Colleges the art of Rugby.

David served the Club in the following capacities between 1970 and 1976:-
  • President 1975/76
  • Vice-President 1973-75   2 years
  • Hon. General Treasurer 1973-75   2 years
  • Chairman - Hockey Section 1973-75   2 years
  • Hon. Treasurer - Rugby Section 1973-75   2 years
  • Tours Secretary - Rugby Section 1972-74   2 years
  • Club Captain - Rugby Section 1971/72
  • On Committee in various capacities at time of temporary disbandment/reformation of Club
  • Selection Committee - Rugby Section 1971/72 )
    1973/74 )   2 years
  • Club Bar Committee 1971-74   3 years
  • Social Committee 1971-74   3 years
