League operated in both 1973/74 and 1974/75 season. Overseas ladies team won in the former and the Wrens in the latter with Overseas runners-up. Other teams were entered by Mtarfa wives, Hospital, and Tal Handaq entered two teams Tal Handaq 'A' and 'B'. Half of the Overseas team was from Luqa. Notable players included Pat Greenwood, Hilary Miller, Norma Lovett and Janet Campbell (all Luqa), Marjorie Riddsale (DOE) and Bridget Lowery. Mrs Ditcham was the organizer. It was all almost loose arrangement started by adverts in Dolphin & Eagle and on B.F.B.S. - but most successful.

Once again I must thank Dave Ditcham and Dave Taylor for details of the Hockey Section, also Dave Ditcham for details of the Ladies Overseas. My thanks also to the Dolphin & Eagle for details of the Joint Services 6 a-side competition and for final results of the league table.
