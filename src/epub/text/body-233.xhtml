Don Alexander

Don Alexander, Overseas and Moseley, served on Overseas General and Rugby
Committees from the time he joined the Club until he left Malta in September,
1980. Don rarely seemed to wish to be in the limelight, but preferred to give
his sound advice in Committee, particularly on rugby matters. His opinion was
always respected. I think, for Don, the highlights of his service to Overseas
were the visits to Malta by Moseley teams. Moseley played good rugby and they
were amongst the best to visit Malta. Don left Malta quietly, giving the
Committee no chance to arrange any farewell occasion in his honour intimating
he was not in favour of such functions. Approximate departure, September, 1980.

Member of Club, 1972/738 years
Vice-President, 1972-80
Bar Committee, 1972/73, 1974-76 & 1977/784 years
Ground work for Moseley RUFC Tours to Malta, 1974/75 & 1977/78

Tim Agius-Ferrante

Tim Agius-Ferrante, Committee Member and Rugby Player for Overseas for
9 years, was honoured in 1979/80 by being elected President of the Club.
Tim was not only the first Maltese President, but also the youngest, in the
Club's history. He took over as President at a very difficult time, when the
Club was fighting for its very existence, and did a difficult job well, until
forced by circumstances to resign.

In his 'younger days' Tim was Head Boy at Tal Handak Combined Services
School and Captain of the school 1st XV. As a schoolboy he occasionally played
for Overseas XVs. Gone are the days. Tim was 'B' Team Captain in 1974/75,
and in the same year he was awarded the 'Young Player of the Year' Cup.

Member of Club, 1973/749 years
President, 1979/80
Hon. General Treasurer, 1978-824 years
Caretaker Committee, October, 1980, to January, 1982
