So ended a great Club.

The Chairman, Cyril Fox, also gave up his residence in Malta and left for home in England in June, 1982. The last of the expatriates. Now, having achieved his 80th birthday, Cyril can sit back and enjoy rugger on the television. Since his return he has received a most kind letter from Mr. R.H.G. Weighill, the Hon. Secretary of the R.F.U.

A copy of Mr. Weighill's letter to Cyril Fox is included in this Final Chapter.

Always first and foremost a Rugby Club, but latterly also a Sports Club, Overseas promoted rugby in Malta for 36 years and it is to be hoped left seeds of the game to be developed in future years.
