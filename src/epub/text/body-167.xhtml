ChairmanT.N. Ricketts
Hon. SecretaryJ.J. Bonner
Hon. TreasurerD. Smith
Hon. Social SecretaryT.G. Jordan
CaptainJ.J. Bonner
Vice-CaptainD. Smith

Again the Soccer Section had a difficult year in putting out a team in both the Major and Minor Leagues. As with all sections of the Club, the Soccer Section was increasingly feeling the effects of the imminent withdrawal from Malta. In spite of this, in the words of the President, Trevor Ricketts, in his end of season report, the Soccer Section, under the general leadership of Jeff Bonner, again managed to fulfil their obligations in the Services' League and equip themselves fairly well, a tremendous spirit spread throughout the whole team. Jeff himself confirmed this in saying that as one can see from results, football was a struggle this year, but I think we all enjoyed ourselves nevertheless.
