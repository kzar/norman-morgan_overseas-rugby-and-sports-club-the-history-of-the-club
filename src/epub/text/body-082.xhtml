Tour Details

Besides the normal tour fixtures, the Tripoli Nomads entered a team in the Inter Service Sevens Championships. Their tour was unique in that one of their members, Mr. I. Churchwood, joined an Overseas Committee Meeting on the 23rd January, 1969, to help to organize their tour, requesting games against the Army (Malta), R.A.F. (Malta) and Overseas XV'S, also to take part in the aforementioned Sevens.

After searching through copies of the Times of Malta and the Malta News I was able to produce the following table. All matches are local teams against touring sides and no normal Overseas weekend matches are mentioned.
