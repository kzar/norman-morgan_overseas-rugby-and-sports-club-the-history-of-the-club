SOCIAL EVENTS 1968/69

With three Touring sides visiting Malta during the season there must have been a fair amount of social activity arranged. This is borne by the fact that Mr. P. Ratcliffe, President, when thanking Colin Brown, Social Secretary, and the Social Committee, for their efforts, remarked that it had been an excellent year. However, except for a Hot Pot Supper and a farewell Cocktail Party at Brian Jackson's residence, both organised for John Evans touring party, very little is recorded.

Once again, the film evenings were the mainstay, with all films and equipment provided by Jack Sharp. All Thursday Club Evenings were well attended.

The Annual Dinner Dance was held on the 29th March, 1969, at the United Services Sports Club, Marsa. This proved to be a most successful occasion. A loss of £4, claimed at the time to be the smallest loss on any Club Dinner Dance ever recorded, was a good effort as a total of £22 was spent on tickets for Official guests.

The Club House remained open during the close season.
