HOCKEY SECTION 1975/76

Chairman              P. Marchant
Hon. Secretary        D. Walker
Hon. Treasurer        P. Marchant
Hon. Social Secretary J. Hughes
Team Captain          D. Walker
Vice-Captain          P. Marchant

The Hockey League 1975/76 was much better organized than in previous seasons. The Overseas Hockey team was also much stronger and consequently it was a successful and enjoyable season.

The League started on the 27th October and ended on the 12th April with 8 teams taking part, namely, Overseas, 234 Signal Squadron A & B, Royal Marines A & B, St. Angelo, R.N. Hospital and Luqa.

During the season the regular players were D. Walker, D. Taylor, P. Marchant, M. Crossfield, H. Ritchie, G. Devey, J. Whitehead, C. Fitches, G. Porter, Captain P. Stanley (O.C. Schools), Miss M. Turner, Miss M. Loughran, D. North, M. Hyland and L. Pape. The last three named were boys from the S.C.S. Tal Handaq. Unfortunately, from this small number of players, the following left Malta during or at the end of the season, L. Pape (February, 1976), M. Hyland (March, 1976), G. Porter (April, 1976), Captain P. Stanley (July, 1976), and D. Taylor (July, 1976)
