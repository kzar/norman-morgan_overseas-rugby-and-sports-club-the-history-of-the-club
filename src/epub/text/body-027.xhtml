ANNUAL GENERAL MEETING

Annual General Meeting held on the 20th October, 1958, at the Queen's Hall, Old College Street, Sliema. Notice of this meeting was given in the 'Times of Malta' on the 15th October, also it was broadcast on 'A' programme of rediffusion in "What's On".

TIMES OF MALTA, WEDNESDAY OCTOBER 15, 1958

OVERSEAS RUGBY UNION

FOOTBALL CLUB

N.C. Morgan, Hon. Secretary, Overseas Rugby Football Club, notifies:

The Overseas Rugby Union Football Club will be holding its Annual General Meeting at the Queen's Hall, Old College Street, Sliema, at 7 p.m. on Monday, October, 20.

All past, present and prospective members are cordially invited, together with anyone interested in club rugby.

Membership is open to U.K. based civilian and Service personnel.

At this A.G.M., the President, Major G.C.D. Scott Lowe, explained that the main reason for the A.G.M. was to put facts before Members and to call for volunteers for all offices. The following Officials, including the 'Resident' Committee, were elected:-

  • President Major G.C.D. Scott Lowe, T.D.
  • Hon. Secretary Mr. N.C. Morgan
  • Hon. Treasurer Mr. H.W. Polkinghorne
  • Hon. Social Secretary Mr. J.E.M. George
  • Team Captain and Hon. Fixture Secretary Mr N.J. Swain
  • Team Trainer Mr. E.H. Stacey (Ex Wasps player)
  • Social Committee
    • Mr. J.E.M. George (Secretary)
    • Mrs M. Rushworth
    • Mr. V.A. Palmer
    • Mrs. M. Polkinghorne

A proposed set of club Rules had been attached to the Agenda of the A.G.M. for members to peruse. These rules were adopted without alteration and have acted as the Club Constitution over the years, only being altered, as expedient, by the required two-thirds majority at an A.G.M.
