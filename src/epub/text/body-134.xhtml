ELECTION OF LIFE MEMBER - TREVOR RICKETTS

At the Annual General Meeting on the 19th September, 1974 Trevor Ricketts was honoured by being elected an Hon. Life Member of the Overseas Rugby and Sports Club. It was an honour well deserved. Mr. C. Fox, the retiring President, said of Trevor 'Your Committee feel that there is a Member of the Club without whose unremitting hard work, helpful guidance and efficient organization we would not be held in such high regard in the sporting world of the Island. No Club can be successful without a dedicated Hon. Secretary and we have such a Secretary who has given up a tremendous amount of his time during the past 6 years in the running of our Club'.

Besides his contribution to sport in general in Malta, Trevor had served Overseas continuously, in one office or another, since arriving in Malta in September, 1967, and played for, or had played for, the 1st teams of all four Sections of the Club. He will always be remembered for his sterling work in reforming Overseas after the temporary withdrawal of the British Forces from Malta early 1972, and for his being the prime mover in Overseas becoming a Sports Club in September, 1973. Trevor also played a great part in training youth and teaching rugby to the pupils at the Maltese Colleges.

Trevor served the Club in the following capacities, up to 1976:-

Chairman Soccer Section since its inception in 1968 8 years
Hon. Secretary, Overseas R.U.F.C. 1969-73 4 years
Hon. General Secretary, Overseas Rugby & Sports Club 1973-76 3 years
Hon. Secretary, Overseas Cricket Section 1972-74 3 years
Served on the Social Committee 1968/69 1971-73 3 years
Captain of Cricket Team 1973-75 3 years
Fitness training of Rugby Section Members Thursdays during Rugby season 1967-74 7 years
