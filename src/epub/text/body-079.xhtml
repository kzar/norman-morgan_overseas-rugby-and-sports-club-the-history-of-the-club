A very unexpected but welcome guest arrived at the Cocktail Party held at Brian Jackson's house. The irrepressible John Evans, and I believe Jacko, together with other members of both 'teams', met Len Hutton and his wife somewhere 'about town'. They were on holiday in Malta. Such an important sportsman had to be invited.

Overseas members of the 1967/68 season will long remember it for the rugby provided, Tours, and the first class Social life. For myself, I regret to say, I left Malta on the 8th July, 1968.
