SOCIAL EVENTS 1969/70

Mr. T.N. Ricketts, Hon. Secretary, reporting in the absence of Mr. C. Charlton (returned to U.K. in July, 1970) said that the Social Life of the Club was not as successful as previous year, the exception being the Annual Dinner Dance, which he described as the 'best we have ever had'.

The only other function reported was a Hot Pot organised at the Club House for John Evans party in March.

The Annual Dinner Dance was held at the Marsa (USSC) on the 17th April, 1970. This was a very enjoyable night, just over 100 attending. The arrangements were made by Mr. Charlton.

The Club House was closed during the month of August.
