Overseas Rugby and Sports Club - The History of the Club

by Norman Morgan

Description

Founded in 1946 by Commodore D. Orr-Ewing and Trevor Lewis, the Overseas Rugby Football Club was the only civilian rugby club in Malta for many years.

This history chronicles the club's development from its post-war inception, through the golden years of matches against Royal Navy ships and touring sides, to the challenges faced during the British Forces withdrawal.

It includes detailed records of officials, match results, and the social life that made the club a central hub for sportsmen on the island.
